About Barangay Malabanban Norte

Barangay Malabanban Norte is located in the municipality of Candelaria in the province of Quezon, Philippines. Our barangay is committed to serving the community and ensuring the welfare of all residents.

Vision

To be a progressive, peaceful, and self-reliant community where residents enjoy high quality of life through participatory governance.

Mission

We are committed to providing excellent public service through transparent and accountable governance, promoting inclusive development, and fostering community participation in decision-making processes.

History

Barangay Malabanban Norte has a rich history dating back to the Spanish colonial period. The name "Malabanban" is derived from local vegetation that was abundant in the area. Over the years, our barangay has transformed from a primarily agricultural community to a progressive neighborhood while maintaining its cultural heritage.
