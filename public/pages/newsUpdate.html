Health Mission Scheduled This Weekend

A medical mission will be held at the barangay hall to offer free check-ups and medicines...

This Saturday, doctors and nurses from the city health office will offer free medical services including blood pressure checks, diabetes screening, and free vitamins for kids and seniors.

Cleanup Drive Success

Residents joined the barangay-wide cleanup drive, clearing streets, canals, and parks...

Thanks to the participation of volunteers, more than 20 sacks of garbage were collected. The barangay captain thanked all who participated and emphasized the importance of cleanliness and unity.
